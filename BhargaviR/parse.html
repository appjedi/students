Disclaimer

Consumers are advised to note the specialty certification information provided on this directory site does not in any way constitute an endorsement or recommendation by the Arkansas State Medical Board, nor does it represent a statement regarding the qualifications or competency in the specialty noted. The information posted in this directory is self-reported by the physicians. Certifications listed may be by non-ABMS boards, colleges or self-designated practice specialties with/without certification through ACGME accredited training programs. Physicians in this state are not required to obtain board certification in order to have a license to practice. Consumers are advised to verify certification directly through the certification board's websites.

NOTE: Specialties are listed in alphabetical order and should not be relied upon to indicate primary or secondary practice specialties. Specialties are self reported and should not be used for credentialing purposes.
